Faculty

At JPIS, we value our teachers, as each one of them not only makes an extra effort to teach in and out of the classroom but also are actively involved in the co-curricular education to every learner. The teachers also play an active role of mentors and give complete guidance to their assign group of students. To enhance the bonding between teachers and learners there are several outing and excursion trips are being organized throughout. JPIS is also having a regular faculty development programs with renowned faculty on various subject matter as a part of their continuing education so that teaching can go beyond class room, curriculum and create a positive environment in the school campus.
